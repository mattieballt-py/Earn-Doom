a chrome extension that works on mobile and desktop.
When certain sites (instagram, linkedin, youtube, reddit, quora, pinterest) but for now, just instagram, is detected, the extension starts counting pieces of content consumed.
This includes posts, stories, or short form videos. The count is displayed in the top right of the screen on an overlay thats draggable. when the count reaches a randomly generated quota, say 10 pieces of content, the box goes from counting to a "Doom reached, create content to unlock more brain rot" and any instagram content on the screen has an overlay mask covering it (if dark theme, dark, if light theme, white).

the + create on instagram will still need to work and no interference or overlay when the user is creating a new post, and once posted, the overlays should reset to non existant, and the counter resets to 0 of 10 and counts again.
